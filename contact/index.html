Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Premium members receive priority support with faster response times, usually within 12 hours.

Custom skin requests are available to our premium members. If you have a premium subscription, you can submit your request through this contact form by selecting "Custom Skin Request" in the subject dropdown, or directly from your account dashboard.

For technical support, select "Technical Support" in the subject dropdown and provide as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing. Screenshots and specific error messages are helpful for us to diagnose and resolve your problem quickly.

We love hearing suggestions from our community! Select "Feedback/Suggestion" in the subject dropdown and share your ideas with us. We regularly review community feedback when planning new features and skin collections.
