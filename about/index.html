Our Story

How a small team of Minecraft enthusiasts built one of the world's largest skin repositories

The Beginning

TurboDraft started in 2018 as a passion project by three friends who loved Minecraft and wanted to create unique character skins. What began as a small collection shared among friends quickly gained popularity in the Minecraft community.

Our founders—Alex, Maya, and Jordan—started designing skins in their free time, sharing them on forums and social media. The overwhelmingly positive response inspired them to create a dedicated platform where players could find high-quality, unique skins.

Growth & Evolution

By 2020, TurboDraft had grown to a team of 12 talented artists and developers. Our collection expanded from hundreds to thousands of skins, and our community grew to tens of thousands of active users.

We introduced our premium subscription service in 2021, allowing us to invest in higher quality designs, better tools, and more artists. This model helped us maintain our commitment to offering both free and premium options to accommodate all players.

TurboDraft Today

Today, TurboDraft is recognized as one of the leading Minecraft skin platforms, with a diverse team of 25+ talented artists, developers, and support staff from around the world. Our collection includes over 10,000 skins, with new designs added weekly.

We've expanded beyond just providing skins to offer tutorials, community events, and custom design services. Our focus remains the same: creating high-quality, unique Minecraft skins that help players express themselves in the game they love.
